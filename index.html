About Us

We serve the vinyl community by finding highly sought-after vinyl records, as well as the most commonly collected artists and titles, and sell them at discount prices. As well as buying and selling vintage vinyl records.



At Neverstop Vinyl, our goal is to restore and preserve vinyl records. We work very hard to thoroughly clean, and find good homes for them. We take pride in our quality and competitive pricing and are very proud of each and every record we offer.



Let us help you build your collection, and save big at the same time. We keep thousands of records in stock at all times. Come see us at one of our sales, or schedule a visit today via call, text, or email.
